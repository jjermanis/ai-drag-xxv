Create a single-page app in a single HTML file with the following requirements:
- Name: Drag Race XXV
- Goal: get your Santa Claus (with his reindeer and drag racer) across the finish line
- the screen in primarily white, like snow. There is a pine forest at the top of the screen, in the background.
- the top of the screen is centered and says Drag Race XXV!!!
- there are two Santa Clauses on the screen.
- there is a finish line on the right side of the screen.
- a Santa Claus is a racing Santa Claus. Starting on the right, there is a line of 8 reindeer. The are connected with
a harness line, like horses would use to connect to reach other. The reindeer face right. To the left of the reindeer is a
drag racer car. Inside the car is a Santa Claus.
- there are two players in this game. Each player has a button that starts the drag racer. Player 1 uses the letter Q.
Player 2 uses the letter P. 
- Each player has special colors for the harness line, the drag racer, and the Santa Claus suit. Player 1 uses a
red special color. Player 2 uses a green special color.
- When a player's drag racer starts, it accelerates toward the right. At first, all you can see is the first deer -
everything else is not seen, to the left of the screen. The players deer and drag racer travel to the right. The first
drag racer that touches the finish line wins the race.
